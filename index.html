Hello!

I'm Hyuna (hyeo-nah), a Ph.D. candidate in the Medical Information Processing (MIP) lab at POSTECH, South Korea, supervised by Dr. Won Hwa Kim. My research interest lies at the intersection of graph machine learning, medical imaging, and computational neuroscience, with particular emphasis on addressing challenges in graphs (e.g., brain connectivity analysis) and longitudinal medical data modeling (e.g., modeling neurodegenerative disease progression). Moreover, my research topics extend to adversarial attack & defense, semantic segmentation, data synthesis, and covariate correction.

During my Ph.D., I am very grateful that my research has been supported by the Google PhD Fellowship and Postechian Fellowship. Last year, I was a visiting researcher in the Department of Psychiatry at UNC-Chapel Hill, collaborating with Dr. Guorong Wu.
